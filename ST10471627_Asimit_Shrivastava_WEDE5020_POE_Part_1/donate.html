Where Your Money Goes

We keep admin lean so more of your rand reaches the field. Below is a typical allocation of once-off and monthly gifts

Indicative split (Target)

  • Project delivery 60 - 70%: This percentage covers plants and materials such as saplings, compost, and soil as well as gloves, bags, litter traps, and repairs.
  • Community and schools 15-20%: Teacher guides, learner kits, workshop facilitation, training / printing, volunteer training, and co-ordernation.
  • Stewardship adn reporting 10 - 15%: Data collections, survival checks, and photography.
  • Operation 5%:Their basic banking payment processing, audits, insurance. We cap core admin to stay lean and mission focused.

Example Use of Funds

  • R250: 1 indigenous sapling + stake + compost
  • R120: gloves, bags, signage for a clean-up team.
  • R90: eco-learning kit for one class.
  • R40: monitoring & data update for tree survival.

Transparency And Assurance

Card Payments are processed securely; we do not store full card details. We conduct financial reviews, and internal checks quarterly. If an error occurs with your donation, contact us within 7 working days for a correction or refund. Personal data is handled responsibly and only to process your contribution and impact updates.
